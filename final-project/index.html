Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary NYC history museum that explores the city's stories through immersive exhibitions, interactive installations, and innovative storytelling, inviting visitors to discover the moments, people, and ideas that shaped New York.

Beyond traditional displays, MonoMuse brings history to life through multi-sensory experiences, from recreated streetscapes and historic interiors to digital archives and augmented reality features. Each exhibition highlights the connections between past and present, showing how immigration, innovation, culture, and urban development have intertwined to create the vibrant, ever-changing city we know today. By blending scholarship with creativity, MonoMuse encourages visitors to engage with history actively, sparking curiosity, conversation, and a deeper appreciation for the forces that continue to shape New York's identity.
