Exhibition 1: Neural Canvas

An immersive exhibition where AI interprets visitors' brainwave data into evolving digital paintings in real time. Using wearable sensors, each participant generates unique visuals that shift with their thoughts and emotions, creating a deeply personal and ever-changing artwork.

A multi-room installation exploring how algorithms shape music, visuals, and online culture through generative art and interactive soundscapes.
