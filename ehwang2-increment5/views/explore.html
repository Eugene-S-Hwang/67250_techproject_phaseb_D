Mission Statement

MonoMuse illuminates the singular stories, systems, and moments that have shaped New York City, revealing how each thread contributes to the city's collective history.

Milestones

  • 2026 - Immersive Neighborhood Exhibits: The museum opens a series of site-specific installations across historic NYC neighborhoods, bringing local history directly to the streets.
  • 2020 - First Physical Exhibition Opens: Debut showcase introduces immersive, story-focused exhibits.
  • 2018 - MonoMuse Launched: Vision established to reframe NYC history through singular narratives. Initially founded as a digital museum led by Dr. Eugene Hwang.
