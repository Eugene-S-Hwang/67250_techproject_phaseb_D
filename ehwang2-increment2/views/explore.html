Mission Statement

MonoMuse's mission is to inspire curiosity and creativity by exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human expression through immersive, innovative, and interactive experiences that transform how people create, experience, and understand art.

Milestones

  • Opening of MonoMuse (2022) - Launched with immersive digital exhibitions blending art and technology.
  • Interactive AI Gallery (2024) - Introduced a space where visitors co-create art with AI.
  • Global Digital Residency (2026) - Began a virtual program for international digital artists.
